Events

We host events every Tuesday. For most of the year the primary focus of these events is a hack session, where members of the Ottawa civic tech community to come together and co-ordinate on projects for civic good. At least once a event a month will also include a presentation from a guest speaker. These presenters are drawn from academia, government, non for profits and the private sector.
